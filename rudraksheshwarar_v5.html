About the Temple

A Sacred Sanctuary of Lord Shiva

Rudraksheshwarar Shiva Temple stands as a sacred sanctuary where the energy of Lord Shiva manifests through the holy Rudraksha. Surrounded by intricately carved stone pillars, ancient archways and the gentle glow of countless oil lamps, this temple offers devotees a space of profound peace, healing and spiritual awakening.
